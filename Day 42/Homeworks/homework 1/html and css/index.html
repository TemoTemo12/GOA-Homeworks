simple Omelette Recipe

an easy and quick dish perfect for any meal this classic amelete combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection optionaly filled with your choice of cheese vegetavnles or meats



preparation time


total:approximately


preparation: 5 minutes


cooking:5 minutes


ingredients


2-3 large eggs


salt to taste

1 tablespaoon of butter or oil


optional filllings cheese deced vegetables cooked meats herbs




instruction

1.beat the eggs in the bowl beat the eggs with pinch of salt and pepper until they are
mixed you can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.

2.heat the pan place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil

3.cook the amelette once the butter is melted and bubling pour in the eggs tilt the
pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface

4.add filllings(optional)qhen the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slighty
runny in the middle sprinkle your chosen fllings over haalf of the amelette

5.fold and serveAs the omelette continues to cook, corefully liff one edge and fold it
over the flings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate

6.Enjoyserve hot with additional salt and peper if needed
